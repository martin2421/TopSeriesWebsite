My top 3 TV Series to watch!






#3 Prison Break

“Keep your friends close, and your enemies closer…”

The world's most dangerous man, Lincoln Burrows, has been jailed and sentenced to death due to the murder of the Vicepresident's brother. On the other hand, Lincoln's brother, brilliant structural engineer Michael Scofield, is convinced of Lincoln's innocence and formulates an escape plan. Together, they'll achieve a Prison Break. Fox's 2008 Prison Break series ended on 2012 with 4 seasons and an immense cliffhanger-ending. However, it doesn't end there. Just last year, Prison Break was renewed for a fifth season with the return of the main characters, Burrows and Scofield, tackling another prison and escape the country.

#2 The Walking Dead

"Sucks, don't it? The moment you realize you don't know sh*t."

Imagine a world where the entire human existence is almost over, where men and women join forces in order to survive, where more than half of the world's population have converted into something else.
This is
The Walking Dead.
In the fight between survivors and the undead, across their 8 seasons and recurring, there WILL be blood and destruction. AMC's The Walking Dead is a post-apocalyptic horror television series about zombies and survival. Our protagonist, Sheriff Deputy Rick Grimes, wakes up from a coma to learn the world is in ruins, and must lead a group of survivors to stay alive. However, the dead are not the only to obstruct Rick and his group during their journey. It is later on in the series that Rick and his group encounter with many different powerful communites. Though, in the world of The Walking Dead, trust no one.

#1 Breaking Bad

"You two suck at peddling meth."

Having the job of a high school chemistry teacher in Albuquerque may just be the borest thing in the universe. Well, think again. Meet Walter White, a struggling high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with lung cancer. Together with his former student Jesse Pinkman, Walter turns to a life of crime by producing and selling crystallized methamphetamine to secure his family's financial future before he dies, while navigating the dangers of the criminal world.
AMC's original series Breaking Bad runs for 5 seasons narrating the story of these two new overpowered men in the mexican cartel and their struggles with family and friends, their secret illegal profession, and their organization. Though sometimes, business doesn't go as planned.
